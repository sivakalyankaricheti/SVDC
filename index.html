SRI VENKATESWARA DEGREE COLLEGE



Introduction
  • Sri Venkateswara Degree College was established in the year 2017 with the mission of offering quality higher education to students, especially from semi-urban and rural backgrounds. Since its inception, the college has been dedicated to academic excellence and holistic student development.
  • The college offers undergraduate degree programs in streams such as B.A., B.Com., and B.Sc. These programs are designed to provide students with strong foundational knowledge along with practical skills to meet industry and societal needs. It follows a well-structured curriculum designed to meet industry and academic standards.
  • Sri Venkateswara Degree College is supported by a team of highly qualified and experienced faculty members. The teaching staff is committed to guiding students not only in academics but also in their personal and career growth.A team of experienced and qualified faculty members guide the students with academic and career-related support. The college also conducts seminars, workshops, and career counseling sessions regularly.
  • The institution emphasizes overall personality development by encouraging participation in sports, cultural programs, and social outreach activities. Career counseling sessions, skill development workshops, and mentoring are regularly conducted to help students face future challenges confidently.Apart from academics, the college encourages students to participate in cultural activities, sports, and social service programs. It focuses on overall personality development and skill enhancement.
