ABOUT SRI VENKATESWARA DEGREE COLLEGE

Sri Venkateswara Degree college Establish in the year 2017 with the mission of offering quality higher education to student, Especiallay from semi-urbon and rural backgrounds. The college offers under graduate degree programs in streams such as B.A, B.com and B.Sc. Sri Venkateswara degree college is supported by a highly qualified and experianced faculty members. The teaching staff is commited to guideing students not only in academics but also in their personal and career Growth

OUR VISION

To be a leading institution of higher education that inspires academic excellence, innovation, and holistic development. We envision creating a vibrant learning community that nurtures talent, promotes lifelong learning, and contributes positively to society and the nation.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to provide inclusive, quality higher education that empowers students with knowledge, critical thinking, and ethical values. We strive to foster academic excellence, innovation, and personal growth in a supportive learning environment. Through dedicated teaching, research, and community engagement, we aim to develop responsible citizens and future leaders.

OUR OBJECTIVE

The objective of a degree college is to offer quality education that fosters academic and personal growth. It aims to develop critical thinking, communication, and problem-solving skills. The college encourages research, innovation, and lifelong learning. It also focuses on building ethical values and responsible citizenship

BSC DEPARTMENT

The B.Sc (Bachelor of Science) department focuses on foundational and advanced studies in scientific disciplines like Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, and Computer Science. It aims to develop analytical, research, and technical skills among students. The curriculum combines theoretical knowledge with practical lab experience. Graduates are well-prepared for careers in science, research, education, and further studies .

B.COM DEPARTMENT

The B.Com (Bachelor of Commerce) department offers education in commerce, finance, accounting, economics, and business management. It equips students with practical and theoretical knowledge of the business world. The program emphasizes analytical thinking, financial skills, and corporate awareness. Graduates are prepared for careers in finance, banking, accounting, and further professional studies like CA or MBA.

BBA DEPARTMENT

The BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration) department focuses on developing leadership, management, and entrepreneurial skills. It covers key areas like marketing, finance, human resources, and operations. The program blends theoretical knowledge with practical business insights. Graduates are equipped for entry-level management roles or further studies like MBA.
